Traits
Lay On Hands - Milnor has a pool of 60 (60/60 remaining) HP he can restore per long rest. He can touch a creature (or himself) to spend these points to restore an equal number of HP, and can expend 5 points per condition to remove the Poisoned, Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, or Stunned conditions.
Protective Aura - Creatures of Milnor’s choice within 10 feet of him gain +5 to saves, are resistant to Necrotic, Psychic, and Radiant damage, and are immune to being frightened
Channel Divinity(3/rest) - Choose 1 for each use:
    1) As a bonus action, Milnor can become aware of every Celestial, Fiend, Undead, and consecrated/desecrated location within 60 feet of himself, for the next 10 minutes.
    2) As an action, every creature of Milnor’s choice within 15 feet of himself must make a DC 18 STR save to avoid the restrained condition.
    3) As an action, Milnor may force up to 5 creatures within 60 feet of him to make a DC 18 WIS save or be frightened of him for 1 min or until taking damage. Such a frightened creature must choose between moving, taking an action, or taking a bonus action.
Spellcasting - Milnor is a level 14 paladin, has spell save DC 18 and spell attack +10.
    Cantrips: Guidance, Sacred Flame
    Level 1 (4 slots) - Divine Smite, Ensnaring Strike, Speak with Animals, Thunderous Smite, Bless, Cure Wounds
    Level 2 (3 slots) - Prayer of Healing, Misty Step, Moonbeam, Branding Smite, Find Steed (1/LR he can cast without a spell slot)
    Level 3 (3 slots) - Plant Growth, Protection from Energy, Blinding Smite, Spirit Shroud, Create Food and Water
    Level 4 (1 slot) - Ice Storm, Stoneskin, Staggering Smite
