Neph’kaz Brightblades
AC 16        HP 48        Initiative +3
Unit			Size 1-5 		
STR	DEX	CON	INT	WIS	CHA		Prof.
 -1	 +3	 +0	+4	 +1	 +0		 +3
Speed 30ft
Passive Perception 11

Traits
Unit - Most values are given in terms of unit Size, which for Brightblades ranges from 1 to 5. As HP decreases, Size decreases to HP/48, rounded up. They occupy an area equal to Size square meters. They have Size reactions.

Actions
Spellcasting - The Brightblades can cast Size spells at once with save DC 15 and spell attack +7.
    Size/day - invisibility, fog cloud, Rime’s binding ice, scorching ray
Area of Effect (Recharge 5-6) - Choose a rectangle of size 16*Size square meters (within 120 ft). Each target inside that rectangle must make a DC 15 Dex save or take 4d6 fire damage, half that on a success.
Individual Shots - +7 to hit, Size targets. 6d6 fire damage to each target
Daggers - This is a unit attack. It affects every enemy unit or creature touching the unit on the front side of the Brightblades. Save DC is 16 and to halve the 4*(side length touching) piercing damage.

Reactions
Counterspell (2*Size/day) - They cast counterspell in response to a trigger.
