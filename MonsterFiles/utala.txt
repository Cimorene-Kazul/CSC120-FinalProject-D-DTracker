Traits
Staff Buffs - Utala has advantage made to avoid or end the charmed condition. Utala has advantage on saves against spells and magical effects.
Lifedrinker - Once per turn, +1d6 Radiant/Necrotic/Psychic damage to a pact weapon hit and heal for 1d8+2.
Eldritch Smite - Once per turn, expend a 5th level slot to deal an extra 6d8 Force damage to the target of a pact weapon hit.
Bardic Inspiration - Utala has 5 bardic inspiration (d8) and regains them after a short rest.
Action Surge (1/short rest) - Utala takes an additional, non-Magic, action.
Luna's Luck (5/day) - Before seeing the effect but after seeing the value of an ability check or save, Utala can add 1d10 to the reuslt.
Spellcasting - Utala has spell save DC 19 and spell attack modifier +11. She can cast the following spells and regains her spell slots after a short rest.
    At Will: Chill Touch (4d10 necrotic on hit), Druidcraft, Eldritch Blast (4x1d10 force on hit), Guidance, Mage Hand, Mending, Minor Illusion, Sacred Flame (dex save against 4d8 radiant), True Strike (+3d6+5 radiant to weapon), Vicious Mockery (wis save against 4d6 psychic), Alter Self, Arcane Eye
    1st Level (4 slots): Bane, Bless, Burning Hands, Command, Healing Word, Hellish Rebuke, Hex, Longstrider, Shield
    2nd Level (3 slots): Darkness, Detect Thoughts, Hold Person, Mirror Image, Pass without Trace, Scorching Ray, Silence, Spiritual Weapon, Suggestion, Zone of Truth
    3rd Level (3 slots): Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Fireball, Hypnotic Pattern, Leomund's Tiny Hut, Stinking Cloud
    5th Level (3 slots): Banishment, Contact Other Plane, Dimension Door, Elemental Bane, Geas,  Hallucinatory Terrain, Hold Monster, Insect Plague, Scrying
    1/day: True Seeing

Actions
Multiattack - Utala makes 3 attacks
Energy Longbow (starts as pact weapon) - Ranged Attack (150/600), +14 to hit, 1d8+6 Force Damage on a hit
Rapier - Melee Attack, +12 to hit, 1d8+6 Piercing Damage on hit
Invisibility - Utala becomes invisible.

Bonus Actions
Bardic Inspiration - As a bonus action grant a creature a bardic inspiration. Once in the next hour they can roll the result and add it to a failed d20 test, to potentially turn it into a success.
Pact Weapon - As a bonus action, Utala can change their pact weapon.
Second Wind (2/day) - As a bonus action Utala regains 1d10+2 hp.
Invisibility - Utala becomes visible again.

Reactions
Cutting Words - When a creature succeeds on an attack or check, or makes a damage roll, expend a bardic inspiration to decrease the value by the bardic inspiration roll.
