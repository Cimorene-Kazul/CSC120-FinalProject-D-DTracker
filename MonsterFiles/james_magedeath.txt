    2/day each: Wall of Force, Dimension Door, Steel Wind Strike, Disintegrate

Actions

Sword Strike - +10 to hit, Melee Attack, 4d10+4 slashing damage and all spells on the target are dispelled. They also lose concentration. If this attack targets something conjured by a spell of level 7 or lower, the spell is dispelled.
