Speed 30ft.

Traits
Bardic Inspiration - Jasilisk has 5 (5/5) bardic inspiration die per long rest. He has the following uses for it:
    1) He can expend one and give it to another creature as a bonus action. That creature can use the die to roll and add to a failed D20 test to try and turn it into a success.
    2) He can expend a die to grant up to 5 creatures within 60 feet of him twice the roll temporary HP and the ability to use their reaction to move their speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
Jack of All Trades - If Jasilisk is not proficient in or expert at an ability check, add +2 to the check.
Beguiling Magic (1/LR or expand a Bardic Inspiration to restore) - When Jasilisk casts an illusion or enchantment spell, he can choose 1 target to make a DC 18 WIS save to avoid being charmed or frightened (his choice) by him. The target can repeat the save at the end of each turn. The effect lasts for a minute or until such a save is successful.
Aura of Majesty (1/LR or expend a 3+ level spell slot to restore) - Jasilisk can cast command as a bonus action without expending a spell slot, then hold concentration for up to a minute to keep an unearthly appearance. So long as he has this appearance, he can cast command again without needing a spell slot on his turn as a bonus action. If the target of such a command is Charmed by him, they automatically fail the save.
