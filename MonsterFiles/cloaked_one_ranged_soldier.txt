Traits
Unit - Most values are given in terms of unit Size, which for foot troops ranges from 150 to 250. As HP decreases, Size decreases to HP/18, rounded up. They occupy an area equal to Size square meters.

Actions
Seraphfrog Spear Strikes - This is a unit attack. It affects every enemy unit or creature touching the unit in a targetable area. Save DC 13 to halve 3*(side length touching) slashing damage
Bolts of Power - Choose an area within 60ft of the casting division of size Size squares. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 DEX save to halve 2d6 radiant damage.
