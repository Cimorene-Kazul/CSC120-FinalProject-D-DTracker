Cloaked One Casting Division	
AC 15         HP 64          Initiative +2
Unit			Size 20-80 	
STR	DEX	CON	INT	WIS	CHA		Prof.
+0	 +2	+0	+0	 +1	 +3		 +4		
Speed 25ft
60ft Darkvision 		Passive Perception 14

Traits
Unit - Most values are given in terms of unit Size, which for the casting division is 20 to 80. As HP decreases, Size decreases to HP/64, rounded up. They occupy an area equal to Size square meters. They can cast and concentrate on Size spells at once and have Size reactions.
Seraphfrog’s Blessing - If you hit the casting division with a melee attack, take 1d6 radiant damage*side length touching.

Actions
Spellcasting - They have save DC 15 and spell attack +7.
    Size/day - Cloud of Daggers, Hunger of Hadar
Seraphfrog Spear Strikes - This is a unit attack. It affects every enemy unit or creature touching the unit in a targetable area. Save DC 16 to halve 10*(side length touching) slashing damage + 10*(side length touching) radiant damage.
In Seraphfrog’s Name! - Choose an area within 60ft of the casting division of size 6*Size square meters. Each creature in that area must make a Wis save of DC 16 or take 6d6 radiant damage. They take half damage on a success.

Reactions
Counterspell (Size/day) - They cast counterspell in response to a triggering spell of level 3 or lower.
