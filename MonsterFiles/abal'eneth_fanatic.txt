Traits
Unit - Most values are given in terms of unit Size, which for Abal’eneth’s fanatics is 50. As HP decreases, Size decreases to HP/100, rounded up. They occupy an area equal to Size squares.
Bezerker Nature - Whenever the fanatics take damage, they must make a Con save with DC equal to half the damage rounded down. If they succeed, halve the damage.
Reckless - All attacks made by and against the fanatics have advantage

Actions
Violent Swords - This is a unit attack. It affects every unit or creature touching the unit on the front or sides. The attacks have advantage. Save DC is 17 to halve 45*(side length touching) slashing damage.
